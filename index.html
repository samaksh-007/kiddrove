What Kids Learn

Learning Outcomes

Real skills, not just theory. Every kid leaves with a project portfolio and confidence.

01
AI Fundamentals & Machine Learning

Understand how AI works — from training models to recognising patterns — using visual, code-free tools designed for young learners.

02
Build & Program a Robot

Assemble a virtual robot and write step-by-step instructions to make it move, detect obstacles, and complete real challenges.

03
Intro to Python for Kids

Learn the basics of Python — loops, conditionals, and functions — through puzzles, mini-games, and creative coding projects.

04
Computational Problem-Solving

Break down complex problems into logical steps. Think like an engineer: plan, test, fail fast, and improve.

05
Design Your Own AI Project

In Week 4, kids design and present their own mini AI application — a chatbot, game, or smart sorter — to family and peers.

06
Ethics & Responsible Tech

Explore questions like "Should robots make decisions?" and "Is AI always fair?" — building critical thinking alongside coding skills.
